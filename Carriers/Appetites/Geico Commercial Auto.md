## GEICO Commercial Auto — Appetite Overview

### WHO THEY TARGET
GEICO Commercial Auto is designed for **small business owners, contractors, and for-hire truckers**. The product spans two main segments:
- **Business Auto & Contractor** – light commercial vehicles used for business purposes
- **Trucking (Local and Long-Haul)** – for-hire truckers, cargo haulers

---

### ✅ BEST / ACCEPTABLE LINES OF BUSINESS

**Business Classes (examples from the product page):**
- Landscapers
- Real estate professionals
- Recycling services
- General freight / dry van (trucking)

**Acceptable Vehicle Types:**
- Light vehicles — cars, minivans, and pickup trucks used for business
- Medium/heavy trucks — catering trucks, cement mixers, flatbeds, straight trucks, box trucks (where available)
- Trailers — utility, gooseneck, dump body, and others

**Trucking segment sweet spot:**
- Local trucking
- Long-haul trucking
- Cargo (Motor Truck Cargo coverage available)
- General freight / dry van operations

---

### ❌ UNACCEPTABLE / AVOID LINES OF BUSINESS

**Business Classes explicitly listed as unacceptable:**
- **Daycare centers**
- **Ambulance / emergency medical transport**
- **Automobile rentals** (car rental companies)
- **Rideshare** (Uber, Lyft drivers, etc.)

**Vehicle/Operation Types with Current Restrictions (from active Client Alerts as of 3/28/2026):**
- **Straight trucks and box trucks — NOT accepted as new business in Illinois (IL), Indiana (IN), Ohio (OH), and Pennsylvania (PA)**
- **Auto haulers — NOT accepted as new business COUNTRYWIDE** (renewals of existing policies continue normally)

*(Note: the product guide contains the full list; the above are highlighted examples.)*

GEICO Commercial Auto is **not available** in:

**Business Auto & Contractor only** (Trucking not available) in select states marked with an asterisk — including **New Jersey (NJ)\***.

- **Standard:** Liability, Physical Damage (Collision & Comprehensive), Medical Payments, Uninsured Motorists
- **Optional Add-ons:** Hired & Non-Owned Auto (BI/PD), Roadside Assistance, Rental Reimbursement, Motor Truck Cargo, Motor Truck General Liability
- **Telematics:** DriveEasy Pro Dashcam — can save trucking customers up to 10%
