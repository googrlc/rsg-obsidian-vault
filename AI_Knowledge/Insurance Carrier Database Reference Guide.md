## Rating scale reference

For agency management system setup, here is the AM Best rating hierarchy:

|Rating|Category|Meaning|
|---|---|---|
|A++|Superior|Exceptional ability to meet obligations|
|A+|Superior|Superior ability to meet obligations|
|A|Excellent|Excellent ability to meet obligations|
|A-|Excellent|Excellent ability to meet obligations|
|B++|Good|Good ability to meet obligations|
|B+|Good|Good ability to meet obligations|
|NR|Not Rated|Not currently rated by AM Best|

**Demotech ratings** (A, A', A'') are accepted by Fannie Mae, Freddie Mac, FHA, and VA as alternatives to AM Best for residential mortgage requirements. Several Florida-focused carriers (Tower Hill, Orion180, Clearcover) carry Demotech rather than AM Best ratings.

This database reflects the most current verified information available. Recommend quarterly reviews of AM Best ratings for any carriers with Negative outlooks or recent rating actions.
