In the world of insurance, agents are expected to handle their business dealings and relationships in ways that meet the universally accepted guidelines that the insurance industry requires of its participants. Also, professional agents will display certain characteristics that not only serve their companies and clients well but also elevate the insurance industry to a level of trust and honesty.

### Chapter Objectives

Upon completion of this chapter, the student should be able to

- examine common misconceptions regarding business ethics,
- understand the benefits of an ethical business culture,
- understand the obligations of agents to their insurers, and
- identify the traits of an ethical agent.
