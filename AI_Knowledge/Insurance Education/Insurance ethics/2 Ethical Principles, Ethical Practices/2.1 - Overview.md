Sometimes good people make bad decisions. This chapter will take a closer look at some of the more common areas of ethical concern to agents. We will examine these areas in detail and show where agents must tread carefully and learn to navigate through any ethical dilemmas they encounter.

### Chapter Objectives

Upon completion of this chapter, the student will be able to

- identify fraud involving clients;
- understand the ethical standards and the companies agents represent;
- differentiate between “hard” fraud and “soft” fraud;
- identify common areas of concern, including compliance, pricing, and commissions; and
- understand advertising and marketing misrepresentation
