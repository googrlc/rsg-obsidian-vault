Today’s agent and agency have more tools than ever at their disposal to ensure good ethical behavior. At the heart of an ethical organization are the expectations by which the company and employees agree to abide. Codes of ethics, codes of conduct, and policies and procedures all serve as both roadmaps and boundaries for those working within the company.

### Chapter Objectives

Upon completion of this chapter, the student should be able to

- understand how corporate ethics requirements impact insurance agencies;
- understand foundations of developing a code of ethics, codes of conduct, and policies and procedures; and
- apply business ethics in various situations.
